On the Geometric Coherence of Global Aggregation in Federated GNN
International Journal of Artificial Intelligence (IJAI) · arXiv:2602.15510
C. P. Kabgere, S. S. Shylaja
Submitted 2026 arXiv
Investigates geometric coherence properties of global model aggregation in federated Graph Neural Networks. Analyzes how heterogeneity in local graph structures impacts aggregation fidelity and proposes principled corrections to maintain geometric alignment across clients in distributed GNN training.

C. P. Kabgere, T. Saha
Submitted 2026

DP-EMAR: A Differentially Private Framework for Autonomous Model Weight Repair in Federated IoT Systems
IEEE International Conference on Communication Systems and Networks (COMSNETS) 2026
C. P. Kabgere, S. S. Shylaja
Published
Presents DP-EMAR, a differentially private framework that autonomously detects and repairs corrupted or drifted model weights in federated IoT deployments. The system applies calibrated noise mechanisms to preserve formal privacy guarantees while enabling self-healing aggregation without exposing client data.

WinTechCon 2025
C. P. Kabgere, S. T. S. B. Sudarshan
Accepted

C. P. Kabgere
Published arXiv

RippleBoards: A Decision Support System for Simulating Social Determinants of Health Interventions to Reduce Premature Mortality in Low-Income Communities
CS 6435 Digital Health Equity · Georgia Institute of Technology, Spring 2026
C. P. Kabgere, B. Rathod, S. Chabbra, N. Sharma
In Submission
RippleBoards is an interactive simulation and decision-support system that models how interventions targeting social determinants of health (housing, nutrition, employment) ripple through community health outcomes. Designed to help policymakers identify high-leverage interventions for reducing premature mortality in under-resourced communities.

C. P. Kabgere
Published
