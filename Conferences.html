Published Paper

FedHisto: Feature-histogram-based Clustering for Privacy-preserving Federated Learning
C. P. Kabgere, et al. • ICDML 2025
Federated LearningPrivacy

Abstract: FedHisto introduces a histogram-based client clustering mechanism for federated training that groups clients with similar feature distributions to reduce drift and communication cost. Tested on multi-sensor datasets, the approach improves convergence and privacy performance.
